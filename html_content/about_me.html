About me:

I'm Raúl Ibarra Aranda, a technical music composer, sound designer, and game developer with over 9 years of experience in the gaming industry. I specialize in creating immersive audio that enhances storytelling and gameplay. Whether composing original scores, designing soundscapes, or working with the latest audio tech, I combine creativity with technical know-how.

I’ve worked with top game studios like Somatone Interactive, Redemption Games, and Globant, on projects such as Power Rangers™ Morphin Legends and Marvel World of Heroes. I'm also skilled in FMOD, Wwise, Pro Tools, Cubase, and Reaper, with a strong background in music theory and sound design.

Currently, I’m pursuing a Master’s in Music Composition for Video Games, and I’m always open to new challenges that push the limits of game audio.
