Angry Bots - Interactive Audio Demo

Top-down shooter game (Unity | C# | Wwise), updated to showcase interactive audio
  • Wwise project setup based on gameplay requirements
  • Composed a suite of 4 music tracks (one for each section of the game).
    • Each section has two layers, where the 2nd layer is activated when you kill all the enemies in the current stage section.
    • Also, each section has an "inside" and "outside" sub-section, which is simpler (just a few bars), so you can focus on the outside ambience.
    • The last section contains a rhythm change, along with a custom 'transition' section to smoothly connect the third section to the last one.
    • Finally, this final section is linked to the 'you win' music, which has the same rhythm but a different melody.
  • Designed and integrated sfxs for enemies, weapons and ambiences.
    • Each enemy has an attack start sfx and a die sfx, with multiple variations, with random pitch changes each time.
    • Weapons (shot and impact) also have multiple variations, with random pitch to improve replayability.
    • The game has two ambiences: Inside and Outside, which switch depending on whether the player is inside or outside the base.
  • Custom components were created to react to different game states:
    • Enemies killed RTPC to activate the second layer of each music section
    • Weapon fire and hit sfx handlers to play different sfx based on weapon type fired and material hit (e.g. rocket hits metal).
    • Footsteps handler to play them based on the material you are walking on (using Wwise switches)
    • Stage section handler, to control which music section to play via "Wwise" state change.

I also converted the game from UnityScript (Js) to C# so I could update the original Unity4 project to Unity 2022.
