Echoes of the Forgotten Isle

Adaptive Music System & Biome-Based Spatial Audio Architecture

Technical showcase demonstrating state-driven music systems, spatial audio optimization, and modular sound design architecture for open-world environments.

Role: Audio / Gameplay Programmer, Sound Designer, Composer
Engine & Tools: Unity, C#, Wwise, Google Resonance Audio
Genre: 3D Third-Person Action/Adventure
Audio Approach: State-driven adaptive music, spatial audio, biome-based ambiences
Technical Audio Implementation:
  • Architected state-driven adaptive music system using Wwise with real-time modal shifts and seamless exploration-to-combat transitions, implementing music state callbacks via C# event system
  • Engineered biome-based acoustic architecture for 5 distinct environments with modular ambient soundscape layers, implementing custom 3D positioning for large complex areas (rivers, waterfalls) more efficient and controllable than AkAmbient Large Mode
  • Integrated Google Resonance spatial audio with custom C# occlusion system for enemy positional cues, enhancing directional awareness and environmental reverberation
  • Designed modular sound system for enemy AI (footsteps, vocalizations, attacks) with randomized variations and pitch modulation, reducing perceived repetition while maintaining memory efficiency
